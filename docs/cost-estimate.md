# Cost estimate

## Phase 1
- Supabase project: $0 to $25/month depending on usage and storage.
- n8n Cloud or self-hosted: $20 to $80/month depending on executions.
- Vercel Hobby/Pro: $0 to $20/month depending on usage.

## Phase 2
- Additional storage and bandwidth costs for images, videos, and audit logs.
- Monitoring and notifications: $5 to $20/month.

## Phase 3
- Serverless functions and custom adapters: $0 to $20/month for execution and logs.
- Optional custom alerting and observability: $10 to $30/month.

## Budget guidance
- Low-cost starter target: under $50/month for light traffic.
- Keep n8n usage low by throttling and scheduling efficiently.
- Move only the most critical workflows to serverless when throughput or reliability requires it.
